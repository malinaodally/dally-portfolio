About Infinity Plus Financial

Guidance You Can Trust, Every Step of the Way

Infinity Plus Financial helps clients make confident financial decisions through professional tax and financial services. We work with individuals, families, and business owners to bring clarity and structure to tax preparation, credit, funding, and financial planning.

Our approach is straightforward: understand your situation, explain your options in plain language, and provide the support you need to move forward — whether that's filing an accurate return, building a stronger credit profile, or accessing capital to grow your business.

What We Offer

Financial Services Designed Around You

From individual tax returns to business funding, our services are built to support real financial progress.

Tax Preparation

Accurate, compliant tax filing for individuals and businesses, prepared with attention to detail from start to finish.

Tax Business Training

Practical training for those who want to learn how to prepare taxes professionally or start their own tax business.

Credit Services

Guidance to help you understand your credit report and take practical steps toward a stronger credit profile.

Business Funding

Support identifying and pursuing funding options to help your business start, operate, or expand.

Financial Education

Clear, practical education to help you understand your finances and make informed decisions with confidence.
